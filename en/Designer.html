Collaboration with Interior Designers

Smart Home technology is not only a convenience for the investor but also a powerful tool in the hands of an architect. Together, we can reduce installation chaos on walls and replace it with minimalist elegance that fits perfectly into your interior vision.

  • End the wall "clutter": Instead of rows of five or six traditional switches in unsightly multi-gang frames, we use a single, integrated control panel. One elegant button can manage lighting, blinds, air conditioning, and light scenes.
  • Exclusive design and materials: Smart switches are available in premium finishes – from brushed aluminum and glass to brass, stone, or natural wood. This makes electrical fittings a decorative element rather than a necessity.
  • Clean walls: Thermostats, temperature sensors, and motion sensors can be concealed or integrated directly into the switches, leaving the space exceptionally clean and consistent.

How long does a Smart Home project take to complete?

The implementation time depends on the complexity of the project and the size of the building. On average, from design to commissioning it takes from a few weeks to several months, aligned with the construction schedule.

Can I control my home while on vacation?

Access to the building while outside its Wi-Fi network range is possible via dedicated devices (e.g., Gira S1) or a VPN server that securely connects you to your home network from anywhere in the world.

Are there any alternatives to wired solutions?

Of course. The choice of technology depends on the stage of the investment:

For new buildings: We recommend the stable, wired KNX standard, which serves as the "foundation" of a smart home.

For finished interiors: We use wireless (radio-based) solutions. Systems such as Simon GO or professional retrofit modules for traditional electrical installations (powered by 230V) work very well here. They allow for quick modernization without the need to cut into walls.

What is the cost?

The budget is highly individual and depends on the number of circuits (lighting points, blinds, heating zones).

In budget-friendly solutions like Simon GO, the cost of a controller is around 200 PLN per point.

In the KNX system, the price range is broader – you can find both switches at a similar price and premium models. We always prepare a quotation based on specific needs and the selected finishing standard of the fittings.
