Your Tailor-Made Home

The KNX system is a global standard for building management, guaranteeing reliability for years to come. Forget about the daily routine – let your home respond independently to your needs, the time of day, and weather conditions.

✦ Scenes and Scenarios

Create the perfect atmosphere with a single click. By setting scenes, you can adjust an entire room to your liking instantly. Leaving the house? Turn off all lights, cut power to selected outlets, lower the blinds, and activate the alarm with one tap.

☀ Weather Dependency

Get weather information online or directly from your rooftop weather station. Analyze the sun's angle and wind strength. Close the blinds in summer to protect the interior from overheating, and let the sun in during winter to naturally warm up your living room.

⏱ Time Schedules

Adapt your home to your rhythm. Raise the blinds with the first rays of sun or pre-warm the bathroom tiles.

Set garden lighting to stay on for only an hour after dusk or open the entrance gate automatically as you approach home.

Full Control in One Place

All home parameters – from temperature and ventilation to lighting and blind positions – are always at your fingertips. Control your home from an elegant touch screen mounted in a central location or from your phone.
